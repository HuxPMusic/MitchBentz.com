About

Hi, I am Mitch Bentz. I make vocal synth covers, archive things I care about, and build small websites. I use he/she pronouns. I am 17 years old.

  • I publish my sites with GitHub and GitHub Desktop.
  • I run HuxP, iWubbzy.com, and The Unofficial Shiki Taigen Blog.
  • I have a twin brother, Safranek (HayBombYT).
